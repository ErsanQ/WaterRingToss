
# Controls

- Left button ◀: pushes rings to the left (nozzle on the right side).
- Right button ▶: pushes rings to the right (nozzle on the left side).

Pressure (PressureEngine):
- Press and hold to build pressure (0..1), release to fire.
- Impulse includes:
  - Low-pressure: larger lateral spread (wobble)
  - High-pressure: small vertical tremor noise
  - Directional bias toward the chosen side

Visual feedback:
- Air stream dots (color intensifies when hitting rings)
- Ripple at nozzle
- Stars and peg glow when a ring scores
- Haptics for success, warnings on device flip ejections
